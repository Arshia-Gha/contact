ISPRS 2026 Paper

Cross-Sensor Robustness and Spatial Generalization for 3D Railway Point Cloud Semantic Segmentation

Authors: Arshia Ghasemlou, Mario Soilán, Jesús Balado, Belén Riveiro

Affiliation: GeoTECH Research Group, CINTECX, Universidade de Vigo, Spain

This work investigates how state-of-the-art 3D semantic segmentation models perform across different railway LiDAR sensors and spatial environments.
